Please note that officially, according to Minor League Baseball, the Mexican League is considered a AAA league. As a result, Mexican League scores are displayed alongside the International League and Pacific Coast League.

A previous functionality, "Last Play", has been removed temporarily because it has been unreliable since the start of the 2018 season.
